About Us

Hello

The Jewel of Norwich

The Bishy Barnabee was founded in 2019 by three entrepeneurs who discovered the dilapidated old estate by chance and instantly saw what it could become. Now fully refurbished, this 17th century estate that used to belong to the Duke of Norfolk boasts grand suites, a relaxing spa and the finest views anywhere in the city. The perfect spot to unwind and get away from the world, why not sink into our luxurious spa pools, book a massage with one of our trained specialists, or get yourself nice and toasty with our saunas and steam rooms. If you fancy a breath of fresh air, why not put your boots on and take a stroll through 22 acres of private woodland, or potter about our ornamental gardens, pefect. The restuarant is a triumph in fusion cooking, the wondrous flavours of India are given new life with a blend of classical french cuisine, it really needs to be tasted in order to be believed!
