Fault report help

Reporting fault in this app is simple, Enter the report fault page through the menu bar or from the home page.

Follow the questions in the form, extra question might appear due to the selections you have chosen. ALL fields shown in the form are required unless stated otherwise. Optionally, you can submit your email to get updates on the progress. If you have already logged into an account, the update will be sent to the account that's logged in.

Other ways to report fault

If submitting fault through the form is not an option, there are several ways to report fault.

  • Report to the staff verbally at the train
  • Contact us by email at contactcentre@greateranglia.co.uk
  • Send us a photograph of the fault on Twitter
